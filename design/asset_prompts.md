# Theme Asset Generation Prompts

Use these prompts to generate consistent assets for new themes.

## 1. Application Logo
**Concept**: A stylized wolf or canine head, representing "Kaiku" / "Kaiku".
**Base Prompt**: "Minimalist vector-style logo of a wolf head profile, looking right. Clean geometric lines, professional tech startup aesthetic, no text."

### Theme Variations
- **Default (Focused Hybrid)**: "Gradient coloring using cyan (#88C0D0) and deep blue (#2E3440). Modern, sleek, flat design with slight depth."
- **Solarized Dark**: "Flat colors using Solarized Dark palette: base02 (#073642) background with blue (#268bd2) or cyan (#2aa198) accents. Matte finish."
- **Solarized Light**: "Flat colors using Solarized Light palette: base3 (#fdf6e3) background with orange (#cb4b16) or magenta (#d33682) accents. High contrast against light background."
- **Pixel Art**: "16-bit pixel art style. Limited color palette. Retro gaming aesthetic."

## 2. Emoji Pack
**Concept**: A set of 6 reaction emojis: Like, Love, Laugh, Surprised, Sad, Angry.
**Base Prompt**: "Set of 6 distinct circular emoji icons: Thumbs up, Heart, Laughing face, Surprised face, Crying face, Angry face. Consistent style."

### Theme Variations
- **Default**: "Modern 3D render style with soft lighting. Glassmorphism effect. Colors: #88C0D0 (primary), #BF616A (danger), #A3BE8C (success)."
- **Solarized**: "Flat vector icons. Minimalist line art or solid fills. Strictly using Solarized color palette."
- **Pixel Art**: "8x8 or 16x16 pixel art icons. Retro RPG style."
