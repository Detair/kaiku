Privacy Policy

Effective Date: [DATE]

At Kaiku, we believe that your voice, text, and communications are your business alone. The very core of our application, Kaiku, is designed around the principles of privacy, self-hosting, and zero intrusive telemetry. It's built on a foundation of "Low-latency voice chat that doesn't spy on you. Open-source, privacy-first, and you're never the product."

Note: This privacy policy is currently a starting point. As Kaiku's development progresses and features expand, all topics regarding data handling and privacy will be defined more precisely.

1. Data Collection and Usage

When you use the Kaiku application:

  • End-to-End Encryption: All relevant communication information (voice, video, messages) is fully encrypted. We cannot and do not intercept, store, or analyze the content of your communications.
  • Metadata Logging: We do collect and log certain operational metadata. This data is logged strictly to ensure the service is running properly, to identify potential bottlenecks, and to continuously improve the software.
  • Zero Third-Party Sharing: We absolutely do not sell, rent, or provide any of your data (including metadata) to any third parties whatsoever. It is used solely for the internal maintenance and improvement of Kaiku.

2. Information on This Landing Page

When you visit this landing/informational page (like the one you are reading right now):

  • Basic Web Analytics: We may collect standard information such as IP addresses, browser type, and time stamps solely for analyzing website traffic trends and page stability. This is completely standard web traffic monitoring.
  • No Third-Party Cookies: We don't embed third-party tracking cookies or advertising pixels. This landing page is simply static HTML/CSS.

3. Third-Party Services

Since the project is completely open source and hosted primarily via GitHub, any interaction you have directly with the GitHub repository (e.g., submitting issues, downloading releases) is governed by GitHub's Privacy Statement.

4. Children's Privacy

The Kaiku service is not directed to individuals under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children under 13. If we become aware that a child under 13 has provided us with personal information, we will take steps to delete such information.

5. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this privacy policy, the security model, or the application itself, please don't hesitate to reach out via our GitHub repository: https://github.com/detair/canis.
